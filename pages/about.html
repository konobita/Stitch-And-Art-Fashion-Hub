About Stitch And Art Fashion Hub

Stitch And Art is a boutique atelier where personalised couture meets contemporary styling. We focus on creating pieces that celebrate individuality while prioritizing fit, fabric, and longevity.

Our Story

Founded by passionate designers, our studio began as a small tailoring shop and evolved into a full-service fashion hub offering bespoke creations, special occasion styling, and seasonal collections produced with ethical care.

We collaborate with local artisans for hand-embellishments and source fabrics with a focus on texture and sustainability.

Meet The Team

Our small but dedicated team includes couture designers, pattern makers, and skilled tailors — each bringing technical expertise and a shared eye for refined detail.
